Project Management


Project management begins, as it should, by defining its subject: A project, according to project management theory, is an activity with three characteristics:


Definite start and end dates
Established resource budgets


Projects : can be large or small, planned and tracked formally or informally, and defined by a legal contract or an informal agreement. They can involve activities that have been performed many times before or entirely new approaches and technologies.
