About

Department of Chemical Sciences- A decade of excellence (2007-2020)

The department offers 2 years M.Sc. program in Advanced Organic Chemistry consisting of 4 semesters and Ph.D. program in different field of experimental and theoretical chemistry.


Thrust Areas @ Department
  • Synthetic Chemistry
  • Supramolecular and Materials Chemistry
  • Catalysis

Vision

" To provide the state of art chemistry education to cater the need of industry and society and also enable students to reach global standards. "

Mission

" To produce well qualified chemist and researcher who are contributors to their occupation by providing to various societal needs. "

Career Development & Placements

  • A comprehensive program that will enable the students to understand the basic fundamentals of organic chemistry and develop necessary skills to apply them to solve any real life problem
  • Choice based basic/advanced level elective courses in diverse fields of chemistry.
  • Opportunity to explore other extensive fields like web-designing, humanities, learning foreign language, etc.
  • Student will train to use Sophisticated Instruments like High-Performance Liquid Chromatography, Microwave Reactor, Infrared Spectrometer, etc.
  • Students will get to learn various synthesis techniques to prepare various synthetic dyes, active pharmaceutical ingredients, pharmaceutical intermediates, etc.
  • Visit to industrial organization/institute that provides analytical service.
