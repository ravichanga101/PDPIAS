Welcome to PDPIAS

The P D Patel Institute of Applied Sciences (PDPIAS) is one amongst the constellation of colleges in the CHARUSAT Campus. It was launched in the year 2007 to impart education and conduct research in emerging areas of sciences such as, Biosciences, Physical Sciences, Chemical Sciences and Mathematical Sciences. A number of sponsored research projects are currently in progress and few are in the pipeline.

Vision

To become an eminent national institute imparting science education integrated with research.

Mission

To engage in education, research, and spread of science for the benefit of society.

Message from Dean's Desk


Welcome to the Faculty of Science, P D Patel Institute of Applied Sciences, Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT), on behalf of our whole faculty, staff, and students. As dean of the Faculty of Science, I am quite proud of the fact that we provide a comprehensive approach to student development through active participation in all aspects of education, including teaching, research, extension, and consultancy.

We provide high-quality teaching, research, and a tranquil environment for those who join as teachers or students, thanks to our highly skilled and devoted faculty. Teaching and learning are important aspects of every academic programme. Its progress is monitored and evaluated on a regular basis as part of the curriculum. Our faculties play an important role in student supervision, personal and professional counselling, and career development. Knowledge is kept up to date by updating the curriculum on a regular basis to keep up with changing industrial and technological demands.

Message from Principal's Desk


A very warm welcome to P. D. Patel Institute of Applied Science (PDPIAS). It gives me great pleasure and privilege to serve as the Principal of this Institute.

The institute was established in 2007, and since then, it has grown significantly in both scope and size. with the intention of undertaking research in cutting-edge fields of Applied Sciences, such as the biological, physical, chemical, and mathematical sciences, as well as playing a key role in the teaching of graduate and post-graduate students. The PDPIAS has made a name for itself as a premier institution in both basic and applied sciences with a substantial footprint over the period of more than ten years.

Read more...

The institute offers excellent resources, well-equipped labs & library, dedicated professors, and ambient green environment that encourage holistic development of our students and assist them in achieving the highest standards in both academics and research. To make sure that every student receives the appropriate exposure and practical learning, we also collaborate with industries, academia and other research institutes of repute.

The academic community here is driven by a desire to establish an innovative, multidisciplinary institution that provides outstanding education, cutting-edge research, and good ethical practices. The Institute is dedicated to fostering brilliance, innovation, and creativity in all of its students. For the benefit of industry and society, multidisciplinary research and development is extensively supported by PDPIAS.

We place a strong emphasis on building rounded leadership abilities. We are pleased to note that the Institute is well-positioned to take advantage of the synergy between interdisciplinary sciences. In doing so, we think the Institute will continue to develop distinctive and innovative programmes to significantly advance the field of science. We have faith that all of our efforts will materialise into noteworthy results that will be recognised in the broader academic community.

I cordially invite you to our great institution to become a member of the academic fraternity. We have a reputation for excellence, but the decision is yours. PDPIAS pledges to use every available resource to help you reach success.

With heartfelt thanks,

Prof. Darshan Patel

Principal, PDPIAS
