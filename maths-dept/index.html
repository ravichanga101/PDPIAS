About

Department of Mathematical Sciences - A decade of excellence

The Department of Mathematical Sciences was established with aim to teach mathematics to Science, Engineering and other Professional Courses students. The university has started M. Sc. (Mathematics) programme since 2017. The Department engaged with research in applications of Mathematics to various fields.


Thrust Areas @ Department
  • Special functions
  • Fractional Calculus
  • Mathematical Modelling in Tribology
  • Applied Mathematics
  • Modelling and computational methods
  • Fluid Flow through porous media
  • Intuitionistic Fuzzy Logic
  • Mathematical Physics
  • Statistical Inference
  • Statistical Computing and Data Science
  • Theory of Special and General Relativity
  • Topological Dynamical Systems
  • Locally compact Groups

Research

  • There are 11 faculty members in the department. 5 faculty members have Ph. D. degree; 8 faculty members have M. Phil. degree; 6 faculty members are pursuing for their Ph. D. degree.
  • The Research fields of faculty members are:
    • Special Functions
    • Fractional Calculus
    • Tribology
    • Compartment Modelling
    • Computational Method
    • Fluid Flow Through Porous Media
    • Statistical Inference and Simulation
    • Fuzzy Set Theory
  • Four students have completed their Ph. D. from the department and at present three students are working for Ph. D. degree in the department. The faculty members have published research papers in journals like: Georgian Mathematical Journal, Communication of Korean Mathematical Society, Annali dell’s Universita di Ferrara, Bolletiontino dell Unione Mathematica Italiana, Journal of Indian Mathematical Society, Journal of Applied Fluid Mechanics, Advances in Tribology, Spectrochimica Acta Part A: Molecular and Biomolecular Specrtoscopy, Discrete and Continuous Dynamical Systems Series S, Chaos, Solitons and Fractals and AIMS Mathematics. Many of the faculty members frequently attend Research Seminars, Workshops and Conferences etc. The department conducts fortnightly research seminars.
